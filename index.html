Bio

Mattias Carosella is a writer based in northern VA, drawing from his interdisciplinary background in international history and philosophy.

His narratives inhabit spaces where cross-cultural folklore meet. Currently, he is exploring character design and world-building in a secondary world that reimagines the Eurasian continent.
