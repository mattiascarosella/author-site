Bio

Mattias Carosella is a writer and recruiter based in New York City. His work has appeared in Andromeda Spaceways, Beneath Ceaseless Skies, and North Dakota Quarterly.

He is currently developing new narratives that blend traditional folklore with speculative futurism.
